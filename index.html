About Me

I have been drawing ever since I can remember. I have always had an insanely creative mind, and loved spilling those creations out on paper. Recently I picked up photography, which has been a nice instant gratification. I love drawing and all, but I never quite feel that they are finished.
I also have a crazy love of cars, and I love making my way around to different car shows. I had the privilage of driving in the BMW Ultimate Driver Experience, and came in 4th place out of my group.
